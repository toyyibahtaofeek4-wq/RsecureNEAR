First Aid for Poisoning

  • Call emergency services immediately

  • If chemical exposure, remove contaminated clothing and rinse skin
  • Keep the poison container for identification
  • Monitor breathing and be prepared to perform CPR if needed

Poison First Aid Guide

×

Swallowed Poison:

  • Do not induce vomiting
  • Call poison control immediately
  • Have the container with you when you call

Inhaled Poison:

  • Get to fresh air immediately
  • Open doors and windows if safe to do so
  • Call for emergency help

Skin Exposure:

  • Remove contaminated clothing
  • Rinse skin with running water for 15-20 minutes
  • Call poison control for further instructions

Emergency Numbers:
